# Metric Definitions

## Daily Transaction Activity

Daily transaction activity measures USDC transaction behavior aggregated by day. It can include transaction count, transfer volume, or other daily activity measures depending on the query.

## Transaction Size Distribution

Transaction size distribution groups USDC transfers into size-based proxy segments:

- Retail
- Small Business
- Mid-Market
- Institutional
- Large Institutional

These labels are proxy categories based on transaction size buckets. They do not confirm the real-world identity of the sender or receiver.

## Estimated Savings vs. Bank Wire Fee

Estimated savings compares assumed traditional bank wire transfer costs with estimated USDC transaction costs.

This metric depends on assumptions about bank wire fees and on-chain transaction costs.

## Stress Period Classification

Stress period classification groups days into normal, moderate-stress, and high-stress periods based on transaction activity and volatility-related thresholds.

## Network Performance During Stress

Network performance during stress analyzes USDC transaction activity during classified stress periods.

## SVB Crisis Case Study

The SVB crisis case study compares USDC activity during the March 2023 banking crisis with a normal baseline period.
